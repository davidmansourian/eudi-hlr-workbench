# Sources and Attribution

This repository contains two pieces of original work — `index.html` (HLR Workbench) and `EUDI_BRIEFING_MOBILE.html` (Engineering Briefing) — alongside a snapshot of public EU material that the workbench reads at build time.

The original work is licensed under MIT (see [`LICENSE`](LICENSE)). The bundled EU material under `eudi_knowledge_bundle/` is governed by its upstream licences — primarily CC-BY-4.0 — and is reproduced here unchanged. See below for source links.

## What is original to this repository

- The HLR Workbench source code (`hlr_explorer_src/`), build script, modular JS / CSS, and the rendered single-file HTML.
- The Engineering Briefing's HTML, layout, prose, role-card structure, and narrative explainers.
- The curated overlays inside the workbench: features, narratives, learning paths, refinement-round delta summaries, hand-crafted Mermaid sequence diagrams for the four priority service blueprints, the cosmos-mode visualisation, the personal-layer + quiz UX.

## What is re-bundled from public EU sources

The contents of `eudi_knowledge_bundle/` — including HLR text, definitions, discussion papers, technical specifications, legal texts, and reference-implementation feature mappings — are reproduced from publicly published EU material:

- **Architecture and Reference Framework** — published by the European Commission's EUDI Wallet team at <https://eudi.dev/latest/> and on GitHub: <https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework>. Released under [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC-BY-4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). The bundled snapshot is unmodified text and figures.
- **EU Regulations and Commission Implementing Regulations** (Reg (EU) 2024/1183, CIRs 2024/2977, 2024/2979, 2024/2980, 2024/2981, 2024/2982, 2025/848, 2025/1569, 2015/1502; Directives 2015/2366, 2016/2102, 2019/882; CDR 2018/389; GDPR Reg 2016/679) — official EU legislation, published on EUR-Lex. Free to reproduce with attribution to the European Union.
- **Reference implementation feature-map.md** — published by the EUDI Wallet GitHub organisation at <https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework>. CC-BY-4.0.
- **PID Rulebook, mDL Rulebook** (Annex 3) — public EU material from the EUDI Wallet GitHub organisation. CC-BY-4.0.

The ARF itself states that it is **informative**. The legally binding instruments are Regulation (EU) 2024/1183 and the adopted Commission Implementing Regulations.
